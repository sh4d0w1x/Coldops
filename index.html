What is ColdOps?

ColdOps is the infra layer behind your cold email. We don’t write your copy or build your offers — we build and maintain the system that keeps your sending alive: IPs, domains, inboxes, authentication and warmup.

Instead of duct-taping random accounts and praying your domains don’t get burned, you get a structured sending machine built to plug into your sequencers.

Why choose us?

  • Infra designed specifically for cold email and outbound.
  • We’ve dealt with burned setups at scale — we design to avoid that.
  • You talk to operators, not generic “support agents”.
